## Lemontron Wiring

You can also reference the Lemontron Klipper config file download to get these wiring details.

- TH0: Extruder Thermistor
- TH1: Bed Thermistor
- FAN0: Chassis Fan
- FAN1: Hotend Fan
- FAN2: Part Cooling Fan
